Who this is for

People rebuilding after addiction, trauma, betrayal, burnout, grief, or spiritual disconnection—who want a faith-respecting path forward.

What you can expect

A steady rhythm: clarity, small steps, honest conversation, scripture-informed reflection, and tools you can use the same day.

Location

Based in Flagstaff, Arizona • Available in-person (when offered) and online.
